Privacy Policy for RevMatch

Effective date: February 26, 2026

RevMatch ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile car-guessing quiz game. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account name, email address, and profile picture. This information is stored securely in our database (Supabase) and used solely to identify your account and display your name on the leaderboard.

Game Statistics

We store your in-app statistics: total score, games played, correct guesses, win streak, and daily challenge completions. This data is associated with your account and used to power the leaderboard and your profile.

Display Name

You may choose a custom display name shown on the leaderboard. This name is visible to other users.

2. Advertising

RevMatch uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data from your device, including your advertising ID, to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account via Google Sign-In
  • To save and display your game statistics and leaderboard position
  • To show advertisements through Google AdMob

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us (see Section 7).

5. Children's Privacy

RevMatch is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including JWT-authenticated API requests, Row Level Security on our database, and server-side validation of all score submissions.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request deletion of your data, please contact us at:

Email: worldly.bussines@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
